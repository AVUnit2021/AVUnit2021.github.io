Descriptions of Discovered Issues

1) Wrong Overtaking Actions

When an NPC vehicle is passing through the same intersection, Apollo predicts that the ego vehicle can pass through the intersection, so it generates an overtaking action, and the ego vehicle does not decelerate, resulting in a collision. Actually, the ego vehicle should slow down its motion and give way to the NPC vehicle.

Video description

When the ego vehicle is approaching the junction, Apollo makes an overtaking action (6s). Hence, the ego vehicle continues accelerating and causes a collision with the 10002 NPC vehicle (11s).
